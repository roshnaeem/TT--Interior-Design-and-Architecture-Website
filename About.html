About Us

Values

An encounter that sets the senses free and traces the colour of every day. The pieces we create at TT are unique masterpieces that cherish within their souls a destination and all the emotions lived during the travel towards their realization.

Brand

A journey begun looking at our native land with new eyes, seemingly suspended in a timeless dimension and untouched by modernity, but which appears to the patient observer as being authentic and of excellent craftsmanship handed down from one generation to the next, the highest expression of Italian know-how.

Philosophy

The philosophy that has led TT since its dawn lays in the negation of these serial and industrial principles, enslaving our lives: at TT, we create a design that embraces the total uniqueness, the idea of connecting each client and each experience not with a piece of furniture, yet with a traveling companion.
